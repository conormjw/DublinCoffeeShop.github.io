Dublin Coffee Shop

About Us

We are currently open 4 days a week (Wednesday – Saturday) as a shop and take away café. The shop stocks produce from local farms and food producers as well as staples and essentials from further afield with a large selection of natural wines, fresh baked daily sourdough and in house made fermented foods. Currently, we are offering a selection made up of daily-changing sandwiches, salads, arancini and soup/lentils.

Our coffee is made with beans from Cloud Picker Coffee Roasters, a Dublin-based roastery who ethically source their beans from Bolivia. You can enjoy the best coffee in Dublin while supporting local businesses and Bolivian Coffee Farmers.
